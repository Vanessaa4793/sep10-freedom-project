Why I chose Basketball as my topic?

I chose basketball as my topic because as I got older me and my dad will go to the park and play where he would teach me offense and defensive skills. As I play basketball now, I use those skills to challenge other teams. I thought it would be a great idea to use basketball for my code and try to make a 3d basketball court. Stay tuned!!
